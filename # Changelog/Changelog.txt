#------------------------------
# Changelog
#------------------------------
# General
- Ludleth's Crown is now a reward from killing the Soul of Cinder without being hit. (Deathless is no longer rewarded in anyway).
- The Advance Journey dialogue now takes place in a menu, and gives you 3 chances to exit before it puts you into NG+.

# Companions
- Added a companion system, where you can summon special NPCs that will follow you about and fith for you.
- To use a companion, you need to find their Incense item, which is used to summon them to your position. This item is reusable, but costs FP.
- You can summon multiple companions at once.
- These are the available companions:
 - Solaire of Astora   - Solaire's Incense: Warrior of Sunlight
 - Lautrec of Carim    - Lautrec's Incense: Mimic - Cathedral
 - Paladin Leeroy      - Leeroy's Incense: Way of White
 - Zullie the Witch    - Zullie's Incense: Pilgrim of Dark
 - Transcendent Edde   - Edde's Incense: Dragon Remnants
 - Sellsword Luet      - Luet's Incense: Treasure - High Wall
 - Steelheart Ellie    - Ellie's Incense: Treasure - Undead Settlement
 - Glaive Master Hodir - Hodir's Incense: Mimic - Ringed City
 - Iron Knight Tarkus  - Tarkus's Incense: Treasure - Catacombs of Carthus
 - Havel the Rock      - Havel's Incense: Treasure - Archdragon Peak

# Infusions
- Lucky now adds damage variance to your weapon. Every attack will have a random chance to gain a damage modifier, both positive and negative.
 - 2%: -25%
 - 3%: -20%
 - 5%: -15%
 - 10%: -10%
 - 15%: -5%
 - 30%: None
 - 15%: +5%
 - 10%: +10%
 - 5%: +15%
 - 3%: +20%
 - 2%: +25%

# Status Effects
- Frost:
 - Cannot be re-applied for 8 seconds.
 - Deals 50 + 5% damage on proc.
 - Reduces stamina by -200 on proc.
 
# NPCs
- Karla is now brought to the shrine after you turn in the Accursed Signet to the Fire Keeper. It is found in High Wall.

# Enemies
- Moved a mimic into Karla's old room in Irithyll Dungeon.

# Bugfixes
- Cornyx now removes the Profaned Pyromancy Tome when taking it.
- Karla no longer appears in Irithyll Dungeon.
- Wave damage in the Trial no longer requires the player to be the source to count.
- Dreams/Nightmares cannot be used as a client player.
